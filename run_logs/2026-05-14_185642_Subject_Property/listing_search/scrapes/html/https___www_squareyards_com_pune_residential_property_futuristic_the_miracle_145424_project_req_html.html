About Futuristic The Miracle

Futuristic The Miracle, located in the heart of Pimple Saudagar, is a state-of-the-art real estate project that redefines the concept of luxury living. With its strategic location, the project offers seamless connectivity to Old Pune Mumbai Highway and Wakad Road, making it an ideal residence for those seeking a blend of convenience and opulence.

The project boasts of world-class amenities and specifications that cater to the modern lifestyle. Residents can indulge in a variety of recreational activities, including a well-equipped gymnasium, and enjoy the peace of mind that comes with a reliable power backup system. The attention to detail in the project s design and architecture is evident in the oil-bound distemper walls in the master bedroom, a testament to the project s commitment to quality and perfection.

Futuristic The Miracle offers a range of luxurious abodes that cater to the diverse needs of its residents. From spacious 2 BHK apartments to lavish 3 BHK apartments, every unit is designed to provide a comfortable and relaxing living experience. With options starting from 92.20 Lac, the project offers an unparalleled level of luxury and comfort at an affordable price.

Frequently Asked Questions About Futuristic The Miracle

  • Q: What are the key amenities and facilities provided by the project?
    The project offers a range of amenities, including a gymnasium, power backup, 24x7 security, party hall, and rainwater harvesting, ensuring a comfortable and convenient lifestyle for residents.
  • Q: What are the available unit options in the project, and what is the price range?
    The project offers 2 BHK and 3 BHK apartments with areas ranging from 710 Sq. Ft. to 1008 Sq. Ft., priced between 92.20 Lac and 1.71 Cr.
  • Q: Are the units in the project RERA-registered, and what is the current construction status?
    The project is RERA-registered with registration number P52100030372 and is currently in the mid-stage of construction. This ensures that the project is compliant with all regulatory requirements and is being developed as per promised timelines.
  • Q: What are the flooring and material specifications used in the project?
    The project uses oil-bound distemper for walls, vitrified tiles for flooring, and RCC frame structure, ensuring a sturdy and well-built living space.
