Points to Consider
What's great here!
Pimple Saudagar is a rapidly developing locality in West Pune
It's a well-connected locality situated right next to Mumbai-Pune Highway
The locality is also linked via Aundh-Ravet BRTS & Pimple Saudagar roads
Bhosari Metro Station on the Purple Line (PCMC - Swargate) is 4km away
Ready 2 BHK apartments make up more than 51% of the inventory
Most of the housing options are priced in the premium budget range
Austin Group & P K Group offer under-construction projects here
ICC Devi Gaurav Technology Park is located about 4 km from the locality
While Pavana & Akurdi industrial estates also fall within a 9 km
Westend and Premier Plaza serve as key shopping destinations within 3km
Proximity to job hubs ensures strong demand from white-collar workers
Pimpri railway station supports suburban rail connectivity
PMPML services and Nashik Phata–Wakad BRTS improve public transport access
GK Gurukul and VIBGYOR Roots schools are located within 5km
Lotus Multispeciality and Maxcare hospitals ensure healthcare access
