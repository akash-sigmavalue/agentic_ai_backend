About Vantage Twenty One
Vantage Twenty One by
Yashada Realty Group located in Pimple Saudagar, Pune offers signature lifestyle amidst lush green surroundings. The project features architectural theme-based apartments in configuration of 3 and 4 BHK flats.
Surrounded by a world of bespoke amenities, including green walkways, barbeque pit and grand entrance, the under-construction project aims to fulfil all the needs of a modern life.
The perfect 3- and 4-Bedroom homes brings in not just ample space for you and your loved ones, but a blank canvas – ready to be shaped into whatever your urban soul yearns for.

About Vantage Twenty One
Situated at a prime location of Pimple Saudagar, Pimpri Chinchwad, Vantage Twenty One is one-of-its kind residential enclave. Vantage Twenty One caters to ever-evolving luxury needs of the urban home buyers.
As residents of Vantage Twenty One, you will gain the space and time to savour a new, expanded scope of life. Whether it’s the bespoke amenities, great expanses of outdoor area, panoramic view of the city, or even the convenient location, life at
this
urban reality project manifests the power of healthy living and enhanced sense of satisfaction.
These iconic luxurious residential units are reasonably priced in the range of Rs. 1. 15 Cr - 2. 35 Cr. Sprawling over 3 acres, the project boasts exclusive 244 units, each symbolizing dexterity and superiority.
The residential units come in a wide range of floor size with 3 BHK Flats offering built-in area from 1042 Sq. Ft. to 1300 Sq. Ft., and 4 BHK Flats offering built-in area in the range of 1843 Sq. Ft. – 1843 Sq. Ft. The well-planned project features 4 iconic towers, brightening city’s skyline with its magnificence. The project commenced on 01 March 2021 and will be available for possession from 01 May 2024 onwards.
Vantage Twenty One Floor Plan
Smart yet modest is what defines floor plan of Vantage Twenty One apartments. Subtle designs, spacious balconies, a cosy kitchen, exquisite interiors, Vantage Twenty One homes present a beautiful balance between social spaces and private. The architects have skilfully integrated bedrooms, bathrooms, living and dining area giving your future home a gorgeous look and feel. The project offers residential units in two configurations and four types of layout designs. 3 BHK Flat is available in sizes of 1042, 1212 and 1300 sq. ft super area and 4 BHK Flat is available in sizes of 1843 sq. ft super area.

Physical Infrastructure
Tucked between Aundh and Baner area, Pimple Saudagar is one of the prime locations of Pune. The location has witnessed exponential growth in past few years due to industrialization, as it is lies in proximity to Hinjewadi which is a major hub for IT/TeS companies.
Pimple Saudagar is well-connected to Mumbai-Pune Expressway via Katraj Bypass and Dehu-road. Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Ltd. (PMPML) provides enhanced connectivity to nearby areas and locality. Pimpri Railway Station is just 3 Km from the area. Pune Airport is located 20 Km away from Pimple Saudagar.
For the ones interested in claiming their slice of the neighbourhood of the future, Pimple Saudagar is the best option.
