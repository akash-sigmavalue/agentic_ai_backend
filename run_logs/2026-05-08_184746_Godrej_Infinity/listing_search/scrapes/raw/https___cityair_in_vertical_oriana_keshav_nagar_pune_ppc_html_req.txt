Overview of
Vertical Oriana
Vertical Oriana, a prestigious residential development by Vertical Infra, stands as a testament to modern living in the vibrant locale of Keshav Nagar, Pune. Established in 2011, Vertical Infra brings 14 years of robust experience to this project, aiming to provide residents with exceptional construction features and amenities. Spanning a total area of 1 acre, Vertical Oriana comprises 4 well-planned towers housing over 200 units, offering a mix of spacious 2BHK and 3BHK configurations. The project's current status is 'Nearing Possession', with phases completed and ongoing as per RERA IDs P52100003700, P52100021565, P52100022830. Its strategic location, coupled with the promise of a comfortable and convenient lifestyle, makes Vertical Oriana an attractive investment opportunity in Pune's real estate market.
