Mantra Mesmer presents elegant residential apartments in Pune

If you are looking for an elegant apartment in Pune, check out Mantra Mesmer. Developed in Keshav Nagar, one of the most sought-after localities for residents, this project comes with 2 and 3 BHK apartments. These homes are loaded with modern amenities, and you will appreciate the elevated standard of living in the estate. With adequate security facilities and lifestyle amenities in the project, you can cherish the happening lifestyle.

Residing in Pune brings you an alluring lifestyle, with a well-developed social infrastructure around your home. For residents, commercial opportunities are plenty, which makes it a popular locality among working professionals. No wonder, the property price at Mantra Mesmer Pune will be rising in the coming years. Investing early in the project, you can benefit from the property value appreciation in the coming months.

The project comes with a large clubhouse, where residents of all age groups can engage in activities appealing to their interests. The estate comes with indoor and outdoor games, a swimming pool, a gym, play areas for kids, and green zones. The apartments are spacious and receive uninterrupted power and water backup. The developers have integrated adequate car parking space into the project. In the multipurpose hall, you can host meetings and events. In the jogging track, you can maintain your fitness without travelling far from your home.

Thanks to the strategic Mantra Mesmer location, residents can get across to the places of interest around the city with ease. Pune has a strong transportation system, which makes it easy to navigate to different parts of the city with ease. You can also reach your workplace in a quick time from these homes. Some of the prominent academic institutions around the estate include The Bishop’s School and City International School. Among reputed healthcare institutions around the complex include Medipoint Hospital and Columbia Asia. One can also reach the Seasons Mall and Amanora Town Center from these homes in quick time. Other prominent commercial zones around the project include EON IT Park and the World Trade Center. Prominent localities like Magarpatta are easily accessible from these homes.

Investors and residents willing to purchase one of these elegant apartments should reach out to 360 Realtors. This is a reputed channel partner of this project and the experts can comprehensively assist you while you purchase the property.
